Frequently asked questions

What counts as a lead?
Each business profile you extract and save counts as one lead. Duplicates are detected automatically and don't count toward your limit.
Can I upgrade from Free to Pro?
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Your existing leads carry over and you get instant access to unlimited extraction and all Pro features.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, bKash, and Nagad. Bangladeshi users can pay in BDT (1499 BDT/month). Enterprise invoicing is available on request.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
Sign up for the Free plan to explore the tool with 20 lifetime leads. Upgrade to Pro when you're ready for unlimited access.
