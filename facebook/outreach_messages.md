# Outreach Message Templates

**Brand Voice**: Authoritative yet Approachable. Visionary but Practical. Confident without Arrogance. Technical but Clear. Never desperate.
**Max**: 150 words per template.
**Placeholders**: `{business_name}`, `{pain_point}`, `{your_name}`
