# Lead Qualification Checklist

Use this checklist to evaluate every lead found through Facebook search queries.

---

## Green Flags (Valid Lead Indicators)

Score +1 point for each green flag present.

- [ ] Business page has fewer than 10,000 followers (underserved, not yet saturated)
- [ ] Page posts are recent (within last 50 days) — active business
- [ ] No website link in bio/about section — needs digital presence
- [ ] Manual ordering process (comments "price?", "details?" left unanswered or handled via PM)
- [ ] Business lists only a phone number or WhatsApp as contact method
- [ ] Post content shows operational pain (e.g., "stock running out fast", "call for availability")
- [ ] Industry matches Skarbol Tech target vertical (retail, manufacturing, beauty, healthcare, education)
- [ ] Business is located in a major city (Dhaka, Chattogram, Khulna, Sylhet, Rajshahi) — easier to serve
- [ ] Page has physical address listed — real business, not a reseller only
- [ ] Owner/manager actively responds to comments — engaged operator

---

## Red Flags (Avoid These)

Score -2 points for each red flag. If 3+ red flags, discard.

- [ ] Page has no posts in 6+ months (inactive/abandoned)
- [ ] Business is outside Bangladesh
- [ ] Page is a meme/entertainment page, not an actual business
- [ ] Scam indicators: no real photos, stolen product images, no contact info
- [ ] Business is a multinational brand or franchise (not decision-makers locally)
- [ ] Facebook page has no phone/email/WhatsApp listed at all
- [ ] Profile is a personal profile, not a business page
- [ ] Industry completely irrelevant (politics, news, personal blogging)

---

## How to Extract Email & Phone from a Facebook Page

1. **About section**: Scroll to the bottom of the page's About tab — phone and email are often listed there.
2. **Page transparency**: Click "Page Transparency" → look for "Contact info."
3. **Posts & comments**: Scan 10-15 recent posts. Many business owners drop their phone number in replies to customer questions.
4. **WhatsApp link**: Look for a WhatsApp button or a number shared in posts — this is often the primary contact.
5. **Google the page name**: Search `"{page name}" Bangladesh contact` — many pages have contact info indexed.
6. **Check pinned post**: Many businesses pin a post with their full contact details.

---

## Scoring System

Rate each lead from 1 to 10.

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 1-3   | Low quality | Discard or archive |
| 4-5   | Marginal | Consider if resources permit |
| 6-7   | Promising | Add to outreach queue |
| 8-9   | Strong lead | Prioritize outreach within 24 hours |
| 10    | Perfect fit | Immediate outreach + follow-up plan |

**Base calculation**:
- Start at 5 (neutral).
- Add +1 for each green flag.
- Subtract -2 for each red flag.
- Clamp to 1-10 range.

**Last Post Date adjustment** (applied after base calculation):
- Within 50 days: no change 🟢 Active
- 51–90 days: subtract 2 🟡 Slow
- 90+ days: subtract 4 🔴 Inactive
- Not found: subtract 1 ⚪ Unknown

### Example

A beauty parlor page (no website, active 2 weeks ago, 5k followers, manual ordering, phone listed, Dhaka-based, responds to comments):
- Start: 5
- Green flags: no website (+1), recent posts (+1), manual ordering (+1), phone only (+1), Dhaka (+1), responds to comments (+1) = +6
- Red flags: none = 0
- Base score: 11 → clamped to **10**
- Post date: 2 weeks (14 days) → within 50 days → no change
- Final: **10**

This lead is a perfect fit — immediate outreach.
